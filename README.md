# Pixel Cat Valley

A Stardew Valley-inspired pixel art survival game where you play as a cat! Fish, chop wood, manage your energy, and survive the day/night cycle.

## Features

- **Authentic Pixel Art Style**: Beautiful pixel art inspired by classic farming games
- **Fishing System**: Equip your fishing rod and catch fish at the water
- **Woodcutting**: Use your axe to chop down trees for resources
- **Day/Night Cycle**: Experience a full day cycle (1 minute day, 1 minute night)
- **House System**: Enter and exit your house with a detailed interior
- **Energy Management**: Drink water to restore energy, sleep at night to avoid energy loss
- **Resource Management**: Collect gold from fishing and woodcutting

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your cat character
3. Press E to interact with objects
4. Press Space to use your equipped tool
5. Press 1 or 2 to switch between fishing rod and axe

## Game Controls

- **WASD/Arrow Keys**: Move character
- **E**: Interact with objects (enter/exit house, drink water, fish, chop trees)
- **Space**: Use equipped tool
- **1**: Select Fishing Rod
- **2**: Select Axe

## Game Mechanics

- **Fishing**: Equip the fishing rod (press 1) and move to water edges, then press E to fish
- **Woodcutting**: Equip the axe (press 2) and move adjacent to a tree, then press E to chop
- **House**: Walk to the door and press E to enter/exit your house
- **Water Dispenser**: Inside your house, press E at the water dispenser to restore energy
- **Day/Night Cycle**: 
  - Daytime (6:00 AM - 6:00 PM): Work outside, collect resources
  - Nighttime (6:00 PM - 6:00 AM): Energy drains if outside, seek shelter
- **Energy Management**: 
  - Energy decreases slowly over time
  - Drink water inside house to restore energy
  - Sleep inside house at night to prevent energy loss

## Survival Elements

- If your energy reaches zero, it's game over
- If you're outside during the night without shelter, you'll lose energy faster
- Manage your time wisely between work and rest
